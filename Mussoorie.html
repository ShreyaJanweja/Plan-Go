The Mall Road

The bustling heart of Mussoorie, **Mall Road** is a must-visit for shopping, dining, and leisurely evening strolls. It runs through the center of town, connecting the two main points, and is famous for its colonial charm and lively atmosphere.

Street Shopping Guide

Lal Tibba (Highest Peak)

Located in Landour, **Lal Tibba** is the highest point in Mussoorie. It offers spectacular, unobstructed, and panoramic views of the snow-clad **Himalayan peaks** (like Kedarnath and Badrinath), which can be seen clearly through a Japanese telescope.

View Telescope Point

Camel's Back Road

This **scenic 3 km road** gets its name from a natural rock formation that resembles a sitting camel. It's ideal for a quiet morning or sunset **walk** and provides stunning views of the valley and the surrounding Himalayan peaks.

Trekking Guide

Kempty Falls

Located about 13 km from Mussoorie, **Kempty Falls** is one of the most famous tourist spots. This large, multi-tiered waterfall drops down from a height of about 40 feet, creating a pool where visitors can enjoy **swimming and picnics**.

Travel Tips

Gun Hill

The second-highest peak in Mussoorie, **Gun Hill** offers a spectacular 360-degree view of the Himalayan ranges and the entire Mussoorie town. It is easily accessible via a **ropeway (cable car)** ride from the Mall Road.
