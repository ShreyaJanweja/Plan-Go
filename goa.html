Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute is a hub of activity, famous for its water sports, shacks, and vibrant atmosphere.

A **UNESCO World Heritage Site** in Old Goa, this iconic church is renowned for its Baroque architecture and holding the relics of **St. Francis Xavier**.

Famous for its relaxed bohemian vibe and the spectacular **Wednesday Flea Market**, where you can find everything from clothes to souvenirs.

Chapora Fort

Known popularly as the **"Dil Chahta Hai" Fort**, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Vagator beach and the confluence of the Chapora River and the Arabian Sea.

One of the largest and most famous temples in Goa, this beautiful example of Hindu architecture is dedicated to Lord Shiva and stands out with its pristine white structure.

Dudhsagar Falls

Meaning "**Sea of Milk**," this spectacular four-tiered waterfall on the Mandovi River is one of India's tallest, especially impressive during the monsoon season.
