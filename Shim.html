The Ridge & Christ Church

The Mall Road

The **Mall Road** is the main street and bustling commercial center of Shimla. Lined with shops, restaurants, cafes, and banks, it's perfect for leisurely **strolling, shopping**, and soaking in the colonial-era atmosphere.

Shopping Guide

Jakhu Temple & Hill

Situated on **Jakhu Hill**, the highest peak in Shimla, this ancient temple is dedicated to **Lord Hanuman**. It features a massive 108-foot-tall statue of the deity and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the entire town and the surrounding hills.

Trekking Route

Indian Institute of Advanced Study (Viceregal Lodge)

Formerly the **Viceregal Lodge**, this historic building was the residence of the British Viceroy of India. It now houses the Indian Institute of Advanced Study and is known for its magnificent architecture and beautifully maintained gardens.

Visitor Information

Scandal Point

This popular junction on the Mall Road connects to the Ridge and is famous for offering stunning **sunset views**. The name comes from a historical anecdote involving the Maharaja of Patiala and a British lady.

Local Legend

Kalka-Shimla Railway (Toy Train)

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the **Kalka-Shimla Railway** is a narrow-gauge track famous for its scenic route through 102 tunnels and over 800 bridges. A ride on the Toy Train offers an unforgettable journey through the hills.
