The Main Temple Structure

Built in the traditional **Nagara style** of architecture, the grand three-storied temple is constructed entirely of sandstone, without the use of steel, symbolizing eternal craftsmanship.

Darshan Timings

Ram Lalla's Idol

The consecrated black granite idol of **Ram Lalla** (Child Rama), carved by Arun Yogiraj, is placed in the Garbhagriha (sanctum sanctorum), radiating divine innocence and power.

Pran Pratishtha History

The Sacred Sarayu River

Flowing near the temple, the **Sarayu River** holds immense religious significance as it is mentioned in the Ramayana. Devotees take a holy dip before visiting the temple.

Ghat Location

Hanuman Garhi Temple

A major fort-style temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, believed to be the protector of Ayodhya. It is traditional for devotees to visit this temple before heading to Ram Mandir.

Jai Hanuman

Dashrath Mahal

The palace believed to be the original residence of King Dashrath, Lord Rama's father. The current structure houses a beautiful shrine to Lord Rama, Sita, Lakshman, and Hanuman.

Palace History

Campus Temples & Kuber Tila

The temple complex includes smaller temples dedicated to other deities like Ganesh, Surya, Shiv, and also the ancient Kuber Tila, expanding the spiritual experience for visitors.
