Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, this sacred temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is the spiritual center of Varanasi, attracting devotees seeking divine blessings.

Temple Darshan

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The most vibrant and active ghat, famous for the spectacular evening Ganga Aarti. A must-see cultural and spiritual experience on the banks of the River Ganges.

Aarti Timings

Sarnath - Buddhist Pilgrimage

Located near Varanasi, Sarnath is the site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon. It is home to ancient stupas, monasteries, and the Ashoka Pillar.

Sarnath Tour

Manikarnika Ghat

The principal cremation ghat, associated with the Hindu belief in Moksha (salvation). It offers a profound, intense, and unfiltered view of life and death cycles.

Ghat History

Ramnagar Fort

A beautiful sandstone fort on the eastern bank of the Ganges, serving as the royal residence. It houses a museum with vintage cars, weapons, and royal artifacts.

Museum Details

Assi Ghat

The southernmost ghat, known for its tranquil environment and morning rituals, including the beautiful Subah-e-Banaras (Morning of Banaras) aarti and yoga sessions.

Morning Rituals
