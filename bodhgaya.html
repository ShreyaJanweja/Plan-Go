Mahabodhi Temple

A UNESCO World Heritage site and the primary temple marking the exact spot where Buddha attained enlightenment. Its ancient architecture is deeply revered globally.

Temple Darshan

The Sacred Bodhi Tree

A descendant of the original tree under which Siddhartha Gautama meditated and achieved 'Bodhi' (Enlightenment). The area is constantly filled with meditating monks and devotees.

Meditation Spot

Great Buddha Statue (80-foot)

An impressive 80-foot high statue of the Buddha in a meditative posture, inaugurated by the 14th Dalai Lama. It is a major landmark and a stunning piece of modern art.

Statue Details

Vishnupad Temple (Gaya)

Located nearby in Gaya, this famous Hindu temple contains the footprint of Lord Vishnu. It is an important site for Pind Daan rituals, highlighting the area's rich pilgrimage history.

Gaya Pilgrimage

International Monasteries

Bodh Gaya hosts monasteries built by various Buddhist countries (Japan, Thailand, Tibet, etc.), each showcasing unique architectural styles and cultural traditions.

Monastery Tour

Sujata Stupa

Built in honor of Sujata, the village woman who offered milk-rice to Siddhartha Gautama, ending his severe austerity and leading him to the middle path to Enlightenment.

Stupa History
