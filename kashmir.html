Kashmir - Paradise on Earth

Explore the stunning lakes, alpine meadows, and snow-capped peaks of this beautiful valley. It's a land of unparalleled natural beauty. ❄️

Dal Lake, Srinagar

The iconic centerpiece of Srinagar, famous for its **Shikara rides**, floating vegetable gardens, and luxurious **Houseboats**. Experience the vibrant life on water, especially during sunrise and sunset.

Book a Shikara

Gulmarg (Meadow of Flowers)

A world-renowned **skiing destination** and the site of the world's highest operating **Gondola cable car**. Gulmarg is surrounded by beautiful meadows, making it a perfect spot for winter sports and scenic views.

Skiing Packages

Pahalgam (Valley of Shepherds)

Known for its tranquil beauty, **Pahalgam** serves as the starting point for the Amarnath Yatra. Its lush green valleys, Lidder River, and pine forests make it ideal for nature walks, horse riding, and camping.

Trekking Routes

Betaab Valley

Named after the Bollywood movie 'Betaab', this valley boasts crystal-clear waters of the Lidder River and captivating alpine scenery. It's a stunning location surrounded by towering hills and thick deodar forests. A must-visit for its sheer beauty.

Nearby Film Locations

Mughal Gardens (Shalimar & Nishat)

The terraced Mughal Gardens, built in the Persian style of architecture, are famous for their cascading fountains, flower beds, and stunning views of Dal Lake. They are a brilliant example of Mughal artistry and landscaping.

Opening Hours

Sonmarg (Meadow of Gold)

Situated in a valley carved by the Sindh River, Sonmarg is famous for its stunning glaciers, particularly the Thajiwas Glacier. It's a base camp for major treks and is blanketed in snow during winter, offering magnificent views.

Glacier Tours
