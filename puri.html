Jagannath Temple

A sacred Hindu temple dedicated to **Lord Jagannath**, one of the *Char Dham* pilgrimages. It attracts millions of devotees and is famous for its massive kitchen and daily rituals.

Temple Timings

Puri Beach (Golden Beach)

Known for its beautiful **golden sands**, clean environment, and stunning sunrise views over the Bay of Bengal. It is also famous for the work of international sand artists.

View Sand Art

Konark Sun Temple

A **UNESCO World Heritage Site** near Puri. This 13th-century temple is designed in the shape of a colossal chariot with richly carved stone wheels and horses.

Explore Carvings

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest **brackish water lagoon** and a major feeding ground for migratory birds. Located a short drive from Puri, it offers beautiful boating and dolphin spotting tours.

Book a Boat Trip

Raghurajpur Heritage Village

A quaint artisan village famous for its **Pattachitra** paintings, palm leaf engravings, and other intricate traditional art forms of Odisha.

Buy Local Art

Rath Yatra Festival

The famous annual chariot festival, where the deities of the Jagannath Temple are brought out in massive, beautifully decorated **chariots (Rath)**. A truly spectacular cultural and religious event.
