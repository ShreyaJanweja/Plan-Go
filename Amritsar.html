Golden Temple

The holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, famous for its stunning architecture, sacred pool, and peaceful ambiance.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historical site commemorating the tragic massacre of 1919, with memorials and gardens for reflection.

Wagah Border

Famous for the daily flag-lowering ceremony between India and Pakistan, a patriotic and energetic spectacle.

Durgiana Temple

A beautiful Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Durga, often compared to the Golden Temple in architecture.

Partition Museum

A museum dedicated to the memory of the Partition of India in 1947, showcasing stories, artifacts, and history.
