ਅੰਮ੍ਰਿਤਸਰ - ਸ੍ਰੀ ਹਰਿਮੰਦਰ ਸਾਹਿਬ ਦਾ ਸ਼ਹਿਰ ✨

ਸਿੱਖ ਧਰਮ ਦਾ ਕੇਂਦਰ, ਇਹ ਸ਼ਹਿਰ ਇਤਿਹਾਸ, ਬਹਾਦਰੀ ਅਤੇ ਸ਼ਰਧਾ ਦਾ ਪ੍ਰਤੀਕ ਹੈ। ਸ੍ਰੀ ਹਰਿਮੰਦਰ ਸਾਹਿਬ ਦੀ ਸ਼ਾਂਤੀ ਤੋਂ ਲੈ ਕੇ ਵਾਘਾ ਬਾਰਡਰ ਦੇ ਜੋਸ਼ ਤੱਕ, ਅੰਮ੍ਰਿਤਸਰ ਦਾ ਹਰ ਅਨੁਭਵ ਯਾਦਗਾਰੀ ਹੈ। **ਸਤਿਨਾਮ ਸ੍ਰੀ ਵਾਹਿਗੁਰੂ!** ੴ

The Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

The holiest Gurdwara of Sikhism, famous for its stunning golden architecture, sacred Amrit Sarovar (Pool of Nectar), and the powerful, peaceful recitation of Gurbani.

Temple Darshan

Jallianwala Bagh

A somber yet crucial historical site that commemorates the martyrs of the 1919 massacre. The memorial stands as a testament to India's freedom struggle and sacrifice.

Visit Memorial

Wagah-Attari Border Ceremony

Famous for the daily 'Beating Retreat' ceremony, a highly energetic and patriotic flag-lowering ritual performed by the border forces of India and Pakistan.

Ceremony Timings

Durgiana Temple (Lakshmi Narayan Temple)

A beautiful Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Durga and other deities. Its architecture closely resembles that of the Golden Temple, set in the middle of a sacred lake.

Temple Architecture

The Partition Museum

Dedicated to the victims and survivors of the 1947 Partition of India. It uses oral histories, artifacts, and multimedia to tell the poignant human story of separation and hope.

View Exhibits

Amritsari Food & Hall Bazaar

The streets of Amritsar are a foodie paradise. Explore the famous Hall Bazaar and taste authentic Punjabi delicacies like Amritsari Kulcha, Lassi, and Langar Prasad.

Food Guide
