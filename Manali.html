Hidimba Devi Temple

An **ancient cave temple** dedicated to Hidimba Devi, wife of Bhima. It's famous for its unique four-tiered pagoda-style roof and location amidst dense **cedar forests**. A site of great religious and architectural importance.

View Architecture

Solang Valley (Adventure Hub)

The primary destination for **adventure sports** in Manali. Enjoy thrilling activities like **paragliding, zorbing, skiing** (in winter), and riding the Solang Ropeway with breathtaking views of the surrounding snow-clad peaks.

Book Activities

Rohtang Pass (Snow Point)

A spectacular **high mountain pass** (3,980m) on the Pir Panjal range. It offers stunning **glaciers** and panoramic views, and is the gateway connecting the Kullu Valley to the remote Lahaul and Spiti valleys. Access is subject to weather conditions.

Check Permit Status

Old Manali

Separated by the Manalsu River bridge, Old Manali is a charming village known for its laid-back **bohemian vibe**. It features traditional wooden houses, narrow lanes, cozy **cafes**, and the famous Manu Temple. Ideal for backpackers.

Explore Cafes

Vashisht Hot Springs

Located in the small village of Vashisht, these **natural sulfurous hot water springs** are believed to have medicinal and therapeutic properties. The springs are housed in a separate public bathhouse near the ancient **Vashisht Temple**.

About the Temple

Beas River & Kullu Valley

The mighty **Beas River** flows through Manali and the Kullu Valley, offering fantastic opportunities for **river rafting** and riverside picnics. The river's banks are also popular for quiet contemplation and enjoying the stunning valley views.
