Radhanagar Beach (Havelock)

Often ranked among Asia's best beaches, known for its pristine **white sand**, lush green forests, and stunning sunset views. Ideal for swimming and relaxation.

Find Sunset Spots

Cellular Jail (Port Blair)

A somber yet crucial **colonial prison** that housed political prisoners during India's struggle for independence. The **Sound and Light Show** is a must-see tribute to history.

Book Light Show

Elephant Beach

The prime destination for **water sports** in Havelock Island. Famous for its clear, shallow water and diverse **coral reefs**, making it perfect for snorkeling and sea walking.

Try Snorkeling

Bharatpur Beach (Neil Island)

Known for its wide, shallow stretch of beautiful water, it is an ideal spot to view the sunrise and enjoy **glass-bottom boat rides** to observe the underwater marine life.

Go Glass Bottom Boating

Ross Island (Netaji Dweep)

Once the headquarters of the British in Andaman, this island now holds colonial ruins, deer, and peacocks, creating a unique historical and natural experience.

Explore the Ruins
