Located in Kashmir, **Gulmarg** is a world-famous destination renowned for its snow-capped peaks, skiing slopes, and breathtaking natural beauty. It is truly called **'Heaven on Earth.'** ‚ùÑÔ∏è

The Gulmarg Gondola

As the highest and longest cable car in Asia, the **Gulmarg Gondola** takes you to the magnificent peaks of Kongdoori Mountain, offering stunning, panoramic views of the Himalayas and the surrounding valleys.

Book a Gondola Ride

Skiing and Snowboarding

Famous for its world-class skiing slopes, Gulmarg is a paradise for both beginners and experienced skiers. The snow-covered mountains in winter are ideal for thrilling adventure sports, including heliskiing.

Skiing Lessons

Meadow of Flowers

The name 'Gulmarg' literally means 'Meadow of Flowers.' During the summer, the area transforms into a vibrant carpet of wild flowers, making it a perfect spot for peaceful walks and nature photography.

Flower Trail

Alpather Lake

This high-altitude lake, located at the base of Kongdoori Peak, remains frozen during the winter and offers a stunning, rugged view. It's a popular destination for trekking enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Trek to the Lake

St. Mary's Church

This small, historic church showcases Victorian-era architecture. Its wooden walls and surrounding green meadows, set against a snow-covered backdrop, make it a truly picturesque and serene spot.

Church History

Gulmarg Golf Course

Recognized as one of the world's highest 18-hole golf courses, it offers a unique golfing experience amidst stunning views in the summer and transforms into a top skiing spot in the winter.

Golfing Tips
